About Us

This website was created as part of a class taught by the University of Michigan ECE department. Engineering 100 Section 430 covers the fundamentals of Music Digital Signal Processing.

The team created this website as part of their final project for the class. We hope you enjoyed learning about the effects listed on this site and played around with the synthesizer!
