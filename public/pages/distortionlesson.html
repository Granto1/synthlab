Distortion

What is it and how we produce it

what is distortion?

While there are many different types of distortion, our synthesizer implements a specific type called harmonic distortion. Harmonic distortion is the technique of adding overtones that are whole number multiples of the sound's original frequency to the original sound.

While harmonic distortion is typically undesirable in various mechanical and electronic contexts (see image source for more information), the effect is a fairly common feature to include in music synthesizers (source).

How is distortion implemented?

For this synthesizer, we run the signal through a bandpass filter and alter the signal based on if the value at a point exceeds the cutoff.
